# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Description

The **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project developed using **Power BI** to analyze customer churn behavior and identify factors affecting customer retention. This dashboard provides valuable insights into customer demographics, subscription services, tenure, monthly charges, and churn patterns through interactive visualizations. The goal of this project is to help businesses understand customer attrition and make informed decisions to improve customer satisfaction and retention.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** is to analyze customer churn patterns, customer demographics, service usage, and billing behavior to identify the key reasons behind customer attrition. This project helps businesses gain actionable insights for improving customer retention, reducing churn rates, and enhancing customer experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- 👥 **Customer Churn Analysis**
  - Analyze churned customers based on demographic factors such as gender and dependents.

- 💰 **Revenue & Charges Analysis**
  - Monitor **Total Charges**, **Monthly Charges**, and customer spending behavior.

- 📅 **Tenure-Based Analysis**
  - Evaluate customer retention patterns based on subscription duration (tenure).

- 🔒 **Service Usage Insights**
  - Analyze the impact of services such as **Online Security**, **Online Backup**, **Streaming TV**, and **Streaming Movies** on customer churn.

- 📊 **Interactive Dashboard**
  - Dynamic visualizations, charts, and filters for detailed customer churn analysis.

- 📈 **Customer Segmentation**
  - Segment customers based on demographics, services, and usage patterns.

- 🧑‍💼 **Senior Citizen Analysis**
  - Understand churn trends among senior citizen customers.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Power BI** – For data visualization, dashboard creation, and interactive reporting.  
- **Microsoft Excel** – For data cleaning, preprocessing, and dataset management.  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- 👩‍🦰 **Churn Distribution by Gender is Nearly Equal**
  - Female customers account for **50.24%** of churned customers, while male customers contribute **49.76%**, showing balanced churn across genders.

- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Customers Without Dependents Have Higher Churn**
  - Approximately **82.56%** of churned customers have **no dependents**, indicating a higher churn tendency in this customer segment.

- 💵 **High Customer Charges Influence Churn**
  - Customers with higher **monthly charges** and **total charges** show noticeable churn patterns, suggesting pricing may influence retention.

- 🔒 **Online Security & Backup Impact Retention**
  - Customers using **online security and backup services** appear to have better retention compared to those without such services.

- 📺 Streaming Services Affect Customer Behavior
  - Customers subscribed to **Streaming TV and Streaming Movies** show varied churn patterns, highlighting service engagement differences.

- 📅 **Tenure Plays a Major Role in Retention**
  - Customer churn behavior changes with subscription duration, suggesting that long-term customers are more likely to stay.

- 📈 **Business Opportunity for Retention Strategies**
  - The dashboard helps identify high-risk customer segments, enabling businesses to implement targeted retention campaigns.
